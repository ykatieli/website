1 Second A Day

Documenting my life, one second at a time
The secret to getting ahead is getting started.

Since the first day my senior year of high school, I have been documenting a second (or two) of my life every day. I am over two years into this journey, and have absolutely no regrets. I have noticed that I am able to remember my days more clearly, and strive to make life more interesting. I do not plan to stop recording the little moments in everyday life, and encourage you to try this out as well. Take out your phone right now, and record a quick second. You'll wish you had started sooner.
