Second A Day

The secret to getting ahead is getting started.

The first day of my senior year in high school, I started documenting a second (or two) of my life every day. I am now in my 4th year of this journey. I found that I have become more intentional about my days, and still love to record the little (and big!) moments. If you'd like, try this experiment out - document a quick second of anything today. You won't regret it :)
