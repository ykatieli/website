SECOND A DAY

The secret to getting ahead is getting started.

The first day of my senior year in high school, I started documenting a second of my life every day. I am now starting my 5th year in this journey. I found that I have become more intentional about my days by recording both the little and big moments. Try it out! Document a quick second of anything today. I've had over 10 friends start doing this with me. It takes some dedication, but you won't regret it :)
