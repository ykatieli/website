About Me

What I Study

Hi, I'm Katie. I'm a 19-year-old sophomore and Jacobs Scholar at Cornell University, majoring in Operations Research & Information Engineering (ORIE), and minoring in Computer Science (CS). I plan to take select coursework in Electrical & Computer Engineering (ECE). I'm from Dallas, Texas and graduated from Allen High School in June 2017.

On campus, I'm an autopilot software developer on CUAir, the Co-Director of BigRed//Hacks, and the Professional Chair (incoming Website Chair) for Alpha Omega Epsilon. I'm the Cornell Ambassador for Sequoia Capital, a venture capital firm based in San Francisco, CA. I'm also a Rewriting the Code fellow.
