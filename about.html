What I Study

Hello, I'm Katie. I am a 19-year-old sophomore and Jacobs Scholar at Cornell University, majoring in Operations Research & Information Engineering, and minoring in both Computer Science and Entrepreneurship & Innovation. I am from Dallas, Texas, and graduated from Allen High School in June 2017.

On campus, I am an autopilot engineer on CUAir, the (incoming) Executive Co-Director of BigRed//Hacks, and the Professional Chair for Alpha Omega Epsilon. In addition, I am the Cornell Ambassador for Sequoia Capital, a venture capital firm based in San Francisco. I am also a Rewriting the Code fellow.

Interests and Fun Facts

Unmanned technology (UGV/UAV), robotics, startups, and venture capital currently pique my curiosity. I absolutely love pandas, Rick and Morty, Ford Mustangs, and watermelon. In my free time, I enjoy bullet journaling, reading, flying drones, and ice skating.

When I was 15, I made it a life goal of mine to visit 30 national parks before I'm 30. I've also roadtripped from Texas-Florida and Texas-California before.
