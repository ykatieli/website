About Me

What I Study

Hi, I'm Katie. I'm a 20-year-old junior and Jacobs Scholar at Cornell University, majoring in Operations Research & Information Engineering (ORIE) and minoring in Computer Science (CS). In the past, I've taken select coursework in Electrical & Computer Engineering (ECE) and Information Science (IS). I'm from Dallas, Texas and graduated from Allen High School in 2017.

On campus, I'm an autopilot software developer on CUAir, the Executive Director of BigRed//Hacks, and the Professional Chair (incoming Website Chair) for Alpha Omega Epsilon. I'm the Cornell Ambassador for Sequoia Capital, a venture capital firm based in San Francisco, CA.
