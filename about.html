About

What I Study

Hi, I'm Katie. I'm a 20-year-old junior at Cornell University, majoring in Operations Research & Information Engineering (ORIE) and minoring in Computer Science (CS). On the side, I've taken select coursework in Electrical & Computer Engineering (ECE) and Information Science (ISST). I'm from Dallas, Texas and graduated from Allen High School in 2017.

On campus, I'm an autopilot software developer on CUAir, the Executive Director of BigRed//Hacks, and the Head of Professional Development for Alpha Omega Epsilon. I'm an Irwin and Joan Jacobs Scholar in the College of Engineering, and the Cornell Ambassador for Sequoia Capital, a venture capital firm based in San Francisco, CA.
